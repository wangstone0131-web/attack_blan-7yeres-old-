# attack_blan-7yeres-old-
Scratch platform. The gameplay involves launching a missile to hit a stick figure. 
The missile is positioned in the bottom-left corner of the game window. When the mouse is clicked, the missile is fired and follows a parabolic trajectory, aiming to hit a stick figure on the right side of the screen. 
The stick figure appears at random positions.

# Implementation Mechanism:
This project utilizes the properties of a mathematical parabola. It first calculates the distance between the missile and the stick figure, then uses mathematical algorithms to generate a parabolic trajectory based on that distance, ensuring that the landing point of the trajectory hits the stick figure. The missile then follows this trajectory during flight, achieving a 100% hit rate. In addition, the peak height of the trajectory is adjustable, allowing simulation of different launch angles.

# Innovations and Highlights:

Applies the properties of mathematical parabolas
Uses algorithms to generate a parabolic flight path
Allows control of the trajectory’s peak height to simulate different launch angles and trajectory shapes
